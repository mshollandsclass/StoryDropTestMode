How to Play
🎯 The Goal

Your team builds a story together — one sentence at a time. Use your vocab word correctly and help your team earn as many points as possible.

✍ Writing Your Sentence

Each round you get a vocab word. Write one sentence that continues the story and uses your word correctly. You have 2 minutes.

🗳 Voting

After everyone writes, you vote on whether each teammate used their word correctly.

Yes — you think they used it correctly
No — you think they used it incorrectly
Not sure — you abstain and earn nothing
⭐ Scoring
Player uses their word correctly +1
Your vote matches the correct answer +1
Your vote is wrong -1
You vote Not sure 0

The game will check if your vote was right or wrong. With 3 players, one word can earn your team up to 3 points.

🏆 Winning

The team with the most points at the end wins. Your teacher will present all the stories to the class at the end.
